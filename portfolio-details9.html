Background information

Background Information: For a university project, I designed a comprehensive 3D model for KAYIIN's mobile cart setup.

The project aimed to introduce authentic Burmese cuisine to the UK market by visualizing an efficient and visually appealing mobile food cart. The design included high-efficiency grills, refrigeration units, portable sinks, and adequate storage, ensuring smooth operations and cleanliness.

I focused on creating a user-friendly customer service area with designated ordering and pick-up stations to facilitate smooth customer flow.
This 3D design was crucial for our pitch to potential investors and partners, effectively communicating KAYIIN's vision and operational strategy.
